From Sloth to Programmer

Time for Change

As a sloth, I tried to figure out what it was I wanted to do. All I knew is it was time for a change. The funny part, after learning about STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Math) through Common Core State Standards, I was one of the biggest advocates pushing it to be the norm for afterschool programs.

It didn't dawn on me at the time that Coding, which is a major part of STEM, would also be a great fit for me as well.

After sitting around completely bored, I had an epiphany. Since I also thoroughly enjoyed the coding games and activities offered to the students, why shouldn't I learn it as well? Here we are!
