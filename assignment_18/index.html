Sensai and his Disciples

Sensai trains his disciples to be fierce and loyal coders. Many a night is spent perfecting the delicate art of front and back end web development whilst balancing organizational skills. I shudder at the thought of how ferocious they will become. For now, they shall remain anonymous.
